Pattern library annotations, documentation, and more 21/11/2013

What started out as a simple chat at Netcel this afternoon between myself and our designer Cheryl on the ways in which we could integrate functional specifications into pattern libraries spawned into a great discussion on the topic via an impromptu Google Hangout.

Thank you Dave Rupert, Brad Frost, Geoff Barnes, Aaron Gustafson and Patrick Fulton for coming online and sharing how they do it. And apologies to all of you that I didn’t make into the Hangout, but a two prong attack from a failed internet connection and a sick child had me beat!
